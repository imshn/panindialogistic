About Us

Pan India Logistics: Reliable Freight and Container Transportation Services

Pan India Logistics is a company that provides freight forwarding and container transportation services with its own fleets and group fleets of around 100 vehicles. It has earned a high reputation with a strong customer base of 75-80 MNCs and Indian corporate companies. The core team consists of trained professionals in the field of Logistics, marketing, finance, and operations, who are trained to upgrade their skills and knowledge base to meet the challenges and demands of customers in a competitive environment.
